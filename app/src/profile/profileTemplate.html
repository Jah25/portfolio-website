ABOUT

I’ve had a love of creation and exploration from a young age and spent my college years studying game design, development and user experience. On graduating I swore an ancient and terrible oath to fight confusing and frustrating design where ever it may lurk.

I’m passionate about games that help us explore who we are; and the power of design to foster empathy, build new tools and create a sense of wonder.

In my work I build user experiences from early concept to launch and beyond. I research user needs, create UI concepts and design user flows to create things that don’t fight the people using them
